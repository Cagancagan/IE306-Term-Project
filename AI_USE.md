# AI Use Disclosure

Generative AI was used as a supporting tool during the development of the Role C component.

It was used to clarify reinforcement-learning and planning concepts, help interpret the simulator interface, troubleshoot implementation and Git-related errors, and improve the organization of experiment scripts and documentation.

The project team independently ran the code, examined the outputs, compared alternative approaches, and selected the final method based on the observed evaluation results. In particular, multiple planning approaches were tested; the final short-horizon look-ahead policy was retained because it achieved a lower mean cost per delivered order than the greedy baseline on the evaluation seeds.

All reported metrics, logs, tables, and figures were generated by executing the code on the provided simulator. The team is responsible for understanding, explaining, and defending the final method, experiment setup, and reported results.
